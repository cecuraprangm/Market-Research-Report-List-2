<p><p>Welding Neck Flanges are commonly available in three types: Raised Face (RF), Flat Face (FF), and Ring Type Joint (RTJ). RF flanges have a raised surface for better sealing, FF flanges have a flat surface for easy alignment, and RTJ flanges have a groove to accommodate a metal ring gasket for higher pressure applications. Each type offers unique benefits based on the application and pressure requirements, making them suitable for a wide range of industrial uses.</p></p>
