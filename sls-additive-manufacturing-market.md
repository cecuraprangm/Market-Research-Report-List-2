<p><strong>The SLS Additive Manufacturing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Selective Laser Melting(SLM)</li><li>Electronic Beam Melting (EBM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electronic Beam Melting (EBM) are two main types of additive manufacturing processes using the SLS technology. SLM involves using a high-powered laser to selectively melt and fuse metal powder particles layer by layer to create complex metal parts. On the other hand, EBM utilizes an electron beam to melt and fuse metal powder in a vacuum, allowing for the production of fully dense metal components with superior mechanical properties. Both SLM and EBM are widely used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and healthcare.</p></p>
