<p><p>3D TOF cameras are categorized into two main types: CMOS Time-of-flight Camera and CCD Time-of-flight Camera. CMOS Time-of-flight Camera uses Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor technology, offering high speed and low power consumption. On the other hand, CCD Time-of-flight Camera utilizes Charge-Coupled Device technology, providing high image quality and sensitivity. Both types are commonly used in various industries such as automotive, consumer electronics, and healthcare for applications like gesture recognition, object scanning, and augmented reality.</p></p>
