<p><p>High Temperature Superconductor (HTS) cables refer to cables made from materials that exhibit superconductivity at relatively high temperatures. The three main types of HTS cables are YBCO cables, Bi-2212 cables, and Bi2223 cables. YBCO cables are made from yttrium barium copper oxide, Bi-2212 cables are made from bismuth strontium calcium copper oxide, and Bi2223 cables are made from bismuth strontium calcium copper oxide. These cables have various applications in power transmission, medical imaging, and scientific research due to their ability to conduct electricity without resistance.</p></p>
