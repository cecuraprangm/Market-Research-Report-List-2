Nitrate sensors are used to measure the concentration of nitrate ions in a sample, while nitrite sensors detect nitrite levels. Ammonia sensors measure ammonia levels, and phosphate sensors monitor phosphate concentrations. Other nutrient sensors may measure different types of nutrients such as potassium, calcium, and magnesium.</p></p>
