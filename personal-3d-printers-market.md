<p><p>Personal 3D Printers are available in various market types including Poly-jet, Fuse Deposition Modeling (FDM), Selective Laser Sintering (SLS), Stereo Lithography, and others. Poly-jet printers use liquid photopolymers to create layers while FDM printers use melted plastic to build objects layer by layer. SLS printers use a laser to fuse powdered materials together, while Stereo Lithography uses a UV laser to cure liquid resin into solid objects. Other market types include technologies such as Digital Light Processing (DLP) and Multi Jet Fusion (MJF).</p></p>
