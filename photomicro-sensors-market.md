<p><p>Photomicro sensors are a type of sensor used for detecting objects or changes in light levels. There are three main types of photomicro sensors: slot sensors, through-beam sensors, and reflective sensors. Slot sensors detect objects passing through a slot between the transmitter and receiver. Through-beam sensors have a separate transmitter and receiver, with an object passing between them causing a change in light levels. Reflective sensors work by emitting light and detecting the amount reflected back from an object.</p></p>
