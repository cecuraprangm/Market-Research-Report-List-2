<p><p>The nail gun market is comprised of three main types: pneumatic nail guns, combustion powered nail guns, and electric nail guns. Pneumatic nail guns are powered by air compressors and are commonly used in construction for their high power and efficiency. Combustion powered nail guns utilize gas cartridges to drive nails, making them highly portable and convenient for remote job sites. Electric nail guns are powered by electricity and are known for their ease of use and versatility. Each type caters to different needs and preferences within the market.</p></p>
