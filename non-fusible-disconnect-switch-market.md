<p><p>Non-Fusible Disconnect Switches are available in two main types: Rotary Type and Knife-blade Type. Rotary Type switches use a rotary handle to open or close the circuit, while Knife-blade Type switches use a knife-blade mechanism to make or break the connection. Both types serve the purpose of smoothly disconnecting power in industrial applications for maintenance or safety reasons. Each type offers distinct advantages in terms of ease of use, reliability, and suitability for different environments.</p></p>
