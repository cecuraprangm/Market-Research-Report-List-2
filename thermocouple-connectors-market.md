<p><p>Thermocouple Connectors come in various types, including Contact connectors and Crimp connectors. Contact connectors are designed to ensure a reliable and secure connection between the thermocouple and the measuring instrument. They are suitable for applications where frequent disconnects are necessary. On the other hand, Crimp connectors provide a more permanent and robust connection by crimping the wires together. They are commonly used in applications where the thermocouple wiring needs to withstand harsh environmental conditions or high vibration levels.</p></p>
