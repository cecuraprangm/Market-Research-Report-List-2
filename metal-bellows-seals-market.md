<p><p>Metal Bellows Seals are used in various applications where static sealing or rotary motion sealing is required. Stationary Metal Bellows Seals are designed for applications with minimal or no movement, providing a tight seal in stationary equipment or piping systems. On the other hand, Rotary Metal Bellows Seals are specifically designed for applications where there is rotational movement, such as in pumps or mixers. These seals provide leak-free performance even when subjected to continuous rotation, ensuring the efficiency and reliability of the equipment.</p></p>
