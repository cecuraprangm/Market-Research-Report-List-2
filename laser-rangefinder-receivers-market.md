InGaAs PIN receivers are known for their high sensitivity and low noise, making them suitable for long-range applications. InGaAs APD receivers utilize avalanche photodiodes for increased gain and improved signal-to-noise ratio. The others category includes receivers using different materials or technologies, each offering unique advantages in terms of performance and application suitability. Overall, these different types cater to a range of requirements in laser rangefinder systems.</p></p>
