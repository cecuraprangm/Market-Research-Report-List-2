<p><p>Floor-mounted painting robots are fixed on the ground and can move in multiple directions to paint large surface areas. Wall-mounted painting robots are attached to walls and are typically used for painting vertical surfaces. Rail-mounted painting robots move along a track mounted on the ceiling or walls to paint large, horizontal surfaces. Other types of painting robots include gantry-mounted robots that are suspended from above and can cover various angles and positions. These different market types cater to specific painting needs and environments.</p></p>
