<p><strong>The Wire Wound High Frequency Inductors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SMD Type</li><li>Plug-in Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wire wound high frequency inductors are available in two types - SMD and plug-in. SMD type inductors are surface mount devices that can be directly soldered onto circuit boards, making them suitable for compact electronic applications. On the other hand, plug-in type inductors have pins that can be plugged into sockets on a circuit board, making them easier to replace or modify. Both types of inductors cater to different industry needs, with SMD type being more common in modern electronic devices and plug-in type being more versatile for prototyping and testing purposes.</p></p>
