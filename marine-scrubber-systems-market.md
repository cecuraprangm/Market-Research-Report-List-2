<p><ul><li>Open Loop Scrubbers</li><li>Closed Loop Scrubbers</li><li>Hybrid Scrubbers</li><li>Membrane Scrubbers</li><li>Dry Scrubbers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine scrubber systems are used to reduce sulfur emissions from ships. Open loop scrubbers use seawater to clean exhaust gases, while closed loop scrubbers use a closed system with water and chemicals. Hybrid scrubbers combine both open and closed loop technologies. Membrane scrubbers use a permeable membrane to separate pollutants. Dry scrubbers use a dry sorbent to capture pollutants. Each type of scrubber system has its own advantages and limitations, catering to different needs and regulations in the marine industry.</p></p>
