<p><strong>The Fiber Optic Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intrinsic Fiber-Optic Sensors</li><li>Extrinsic Fiber-Optic Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber optic sensors are categorized into two main types: Intrinsic and Extrinsic sensors. Intrinsic fiber-optic sensors have the sensing element integrated within the fiber itself, allowing for direct measurement of the desired parameter. On the other hand, Extrinsic fiber-optic sensors use external probes to detect the variations in the optical signal traveling through the fiber. Both types offer unique advantages in various applications, such as in industries like healthcare, aerospace, and telecommunications, where accurate and reliable sensing capabilities are crucial.</p></p>
