<p><p>Threaded couplings are fittings used to connect two pipes or tubing together. Threaded half couplings have threads on only one end and are used to join a pipe to a fitting or another pipe. Threaded full couplings have threads on both ends and are used to join two pipes together directly. Both types are commonly used in plumbing, oil and gas, and other industrial applications where a secure and leak-proof connection is needed.</p></p>
