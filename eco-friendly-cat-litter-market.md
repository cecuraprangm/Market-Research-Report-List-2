<p><p>Eco-friendly cat litter market is categorized into two main types: non-clumping and clumping. Non-clumping litter is typically made from natural materials like paper, wood, or corn, and absorbs moisture without forming solid clumps. This type of litter is usually more budget-friendly and easier to clean up. On the other hand, clumping litter forms solid clumps when it comes into contact with moisture, making it easier to scoop out waste. While clumping litter is often preferred for its convenience, non-clumping litter is favored for its eco-friendly composition.</p></p>
