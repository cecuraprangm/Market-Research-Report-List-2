<p><ul><li>Valve Regulated Type</li><li>Rechargeable Sealed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Maintenance-free lead-acid batteries come in two main types: valve regulated type and rechargeable sealed type. Valve regulated batteries are designed with a valve that allows gases to escape, while sealing the unit to prevent electrolyte leakage. These batteries are commonly used in applications where maintenance is difficult. Rechargeable sealed batteries are tightly sealed to prevent electrolyte evaporation and are designed to be recharged multiple times. Both types of batteries offer convenience and ease of use in various applications.</p></p>
