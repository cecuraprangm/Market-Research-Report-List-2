<p><p>Wireless blood glucose meters have two main types in the market: manual blood glucose meters and automatic blood glucose meters. Manual blood glucose meters require the user to physically draw a blood sample and place it on a test strip to measure glucose levels. On the other hand, automatic blood glucose meters only require the user to insert a test strip and the meter will automatically draw and analyze the blood sample. Both types offer convenience and ease of use for individuals monitoring their blood glucose levels.</p></p>
