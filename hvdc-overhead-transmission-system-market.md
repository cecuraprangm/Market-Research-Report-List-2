<p><p>HVDC overhead transmission systems are classified into three main types: point-to-point transmission, back-to-back station, and multi-terminal system. </p><p>1. Point-to-point transmission involves the direct transmission of power from one point to another over long distances with minimal losses.</p><p>2. Back-to-back stations are used to connect two AC systems with different frequencies or voltage levels.</p><p>3. Multi-terminal systems involve the connection of more than two points to enable power exchange between multiple locations efficiently. Each type serves different purposes in the HVDC transmission market.</p></p>
