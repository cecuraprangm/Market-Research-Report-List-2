Vertical screw pumps are designed to handle liquids with high viscosity and solids content, typically used in wastewater treatment plants. Horizontal screw pumps are more versatile and can handle a wider range of applications, making them popular in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals. Both types offer efficient and hygienic pumping solutions for sanitary applications.</p></p>
