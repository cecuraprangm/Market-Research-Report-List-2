<p><p>High Frequency Surface Mount Inductors are available in three main types - Wire Wound Type, Film Type, and Multilayer Type. Wire Wound Type inductors are made of wire wound around a core and are suitable for high power applications. Film Type inductors use a thin film technology for improved performance and efficiency. Multilayer Type inductors are constructed with multiple layers of inductor coils for high inductance values in a compact size. Each type offers unique advantages for different high frequency applications.</p></p>
