<p><p>Miniature attenuators are electronic devices that reduce the amplitude of a signal without affecting its waveform. There are two main types of miniature attenuators in the market: variable and fixed. Variable attenuators allow users to adjust the level of attenuation, making them suitable for situations where signal strength needs to be easily controlled. Fixed attenuators, on the other hand, offer a set level of attenuation and are ideal for applications where a consistent reduction in signal strength is required.</p></p>
