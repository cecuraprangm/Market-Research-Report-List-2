<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Uncooled Infrared Microbolometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vox Microbolometer</li><li>α-Si Microbolometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Uncooled infrared microbolometers are classified into two main types: Vox microbolometer and α-Si microbolometer. Vox microbolometers use vanadium oxide as the sensing material, offering high sensitivity and performance. They are widely used in thermal imaging cameras and other applications requiring high accuracy. On the other hand, α-Si microbolometers use amorphous silicon as the sensing material, providing cost-effective solutions with decent performance. Both types cater to different market needs, with Vox microbolometers dominating in high-end applications and α-Si microbolometers in cost-sensitive markets.</p></p>
