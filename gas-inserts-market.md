<p><ul><li>Vent-Free</li><li>Direct Vent</li><li>Natural Vent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas inserts are available in three main market types: vent-free, direct vent, and natural vent. Vent-free inserts do not require a chimney or venting system, making them a convenient option for homeowners without existing venting. Direct vent inserts utilize a sealed combustion system, drawing air from outside for combustion and venting exhaust gases outside. Natural vent inserts use an existing chimney for venting, relying on the natural draft of warm air rising. Each market type offers unique advantages and considerations for consumers to choose from.</p></p>
