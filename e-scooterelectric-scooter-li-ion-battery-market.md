<p><ul><li>Nickel Metal Hydride Battery (NiMH)</li><li>Sealed Lead Acid Battery (SLA)</li><li>Lithium Ion Battery (Li-ion, LFP, LiPo)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>E-scooter batteries can be classified into three main types - Nickel Metal Hydride Battery (NiMH), Sealed Lead Acid Battery (SLA), and Lithium Ion Battery (Li-ion, LFP, LiPo). NiMH batteries offer good energy density but are heavier and less efficient. SLA batteries are heavier, have lower energy density, and shorter lifespan. Li-ion batteries are the most widely used in e-scooters due to their high energy density, lighter weight, and longer lifespan. Within Li-ion category, Lithium Iron Phosphate (LFP) and Lithium Polymer (LiPo) batteries offer different trade-offs in terms of safety, energy density, and lifespan.</p></p>
