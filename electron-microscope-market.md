<p><p>Electron microscopy market includes two main types: Scanning Electron Microscopy (SEM) and Transmission Electron Microscopy (TEM). SEM is used to study the surface of a specimen by scanning it with a focused beam of electrons to create a detailed 3D image. TEM, on the other hand, allows for the study of the internal structure of a specimen by transmitting electrons through thin sections of the sample. Both types of electron microscopy play a crucial role in various scientific disciplines, such as biology, materials science, and nanotechnology.</p></p>
