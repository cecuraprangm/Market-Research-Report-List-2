<p><p>Vacuum Gauges for Semiconductor Equipment market consists of various types such as Capacitance Diaphragm Gauge, Ionization Vacuum Gauge, Pirani Vacuum Gauge, and others. Capacitance Diaphragm Gauge measures pressure by detecting the change in capacitance in a diaphragm due to pressure variation. Ionization Vacuum Gauge measures pressure by ionizing gas molecules and measuring the resulting electrical current. Pirani Vacuum Gauge functions by detecting the change in resistance of a heated wire due to gas pressure. Other types include Penning Gauge, Thermocouple Gauge, and Cold Cathode Gauge, among others.</p></p>
