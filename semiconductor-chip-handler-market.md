<p><strong>The Semiconductor Chip Handler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pick-and-Place Handlers</li><li>Turret Handlers</li><li>Gravity Handlers</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor chip handlers are used in the manufacturing process to pick and place semiconductor chips onto boards. Pick-and-place handlers use robotic arms to accurately place chips, while turret handlers utilize a rotating turret for high-speed operation. Gravity handlers rely on gravity to feed chips onto boards without the need for complex mechanisms. Other types of handlers may include tape-and-reel handlers, test handlers, or laser handlers. Each type has its own advantages and is suited to different manufacturing requirements.</p></p>
