<p><p>3D imaging and sensing components market includes various sensing technologies such as stereo vision, structured light, and time-of-flight (ToF). Stereo vision technology uses two cameras to create a 3D image by analyzing the disparity between the images. Structured light technology projects a pattern onto an object and analyzes the distortions to create a 3D image. ToF technology measures the time taken for light to bounce back from an object to determine its distance, enabling accurate depth sensing in various applications.</p></p>
