<p><p>Burner nozzles are categorized into two main types in the market: standard nozzles and twin filter nozzles. Standard nozzles are widely used for general applications due to their simplicity and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, twin filter nozzles are designed with a filtration system to prevent clogging and ensure a consistent spray pattern. Twin filter nozzles are preferred for applications where the quality of the spray is crucial and need to operate at high pressures, such as in industrial settings.</p></p>
