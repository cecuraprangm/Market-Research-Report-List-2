<p><ul><li>Melting Alloy Thermal Overload Relay</li><li>Bimetallic Strip Thermal Overload Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal Overload Relays are protective devices used to prevent overheating in electrical circuits. There are two main types: Melting Alloy Thermal Overload Relays and Bimetallic Strip Thermal Overload Relays. Melting Alloy relays use a heating element to melt and open the circuit during overload conditions, while Bimetallic Strip relays rely on the differential expansion of two metals to detect and respond to excessive heat. Both types are crucial for ensuring the safety and efficiency of electrical systems.</p></p>
