<p><p>High-index plastic lenses are lenses that are thinner and lighter than traditional lenses due to their higher refractive index. The market is divided into three main types based on their refractive index: 1.61 index, 1.67 index, and 1.74 index. The 1.61 index lenses are the most common high-index lenses and provide a good balance of thinness and affordability. The 1.67 index lenses offer even thinner and lighter lenses, while the 1.74 index lenses are the thinnest and most lightweight option available, but also the most expensive.</p></p>
