<p><p>4D printing technology market is categorized into various types, including Inkjet Printing, Melt Deposition Modeling (FDM), Direct Ink Writing (DIW), Stereolithography (SLA), Electron Beam Melting (EBM), and Selective Laser Melting (SLM). Inkjet Printing utilizes droplets of material to create layers, while FDM involves melting a thermoplastic filament to build the object. DIW involves the extrusion of material through a nozzle, SLA uses a UV laser to solidify resin, EBM utilizes an electron beam to melt metal powder, and SLM uses a laser to fuse metal powder.</p></p>
