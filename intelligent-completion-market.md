<p><p>Intelligent Completion market consists of four main types: Downhole Monitoring System, Surface Control System, Downhole Control System, and Communication System. The Downhole Monitoring System allows operators to monitor reservoir and well conditions in real-time. The Surface Control System is used to control the flow of production and injection fluids at the surface. The Downhole Control System enables remote adjustments to well settings without intervention. The Communication System facilitates data exchange between downhole and surface equipment for efficient well management.</p></p>
