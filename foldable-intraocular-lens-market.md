<p><ul><li>Hydrophobic Acrylic IOLs</li><li>Hydrophilic Acrylic IOLs</li><li>Other Foldable IOLs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Foldable Intraocular Lens (IOL) market types include Hydrophobic Acrylic IOLs, Hydrophilic Acrylic IOLs, and Other Foldable IOLs. Hydrophobic Acrylic IOLs are made of materials that repel water, reducing the risk of post-operative inflammation and maintaining clarity. Hydrophilic Acrylic IOLs are made of materials that attract water, providing flexibility and better positioning within the eye. Other Foldable IOLs encompass a range of materials such as silicone or collamer, offering different benefits like reduced glare and improved contrast sensitivity.</p></p>
