<p><strong>The Gauze Wound Dressing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Woven Gauze</li><li>Nonwoven Gauze</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gauze wound dressing market consists of two main types: woven gauze and nonwoven gauze. Woven gauze is made by interlacing threads in a crisscross pattern, providing strength and durability. Nonwoven gauze, on the other hand, is made by pressing fibers together without weaving, making it softer and more absorbent. Both types of gauze are commonly used in medical settings for wound care, with woven gauze often preferred for larger wounds and nonwoven gauze for more delicate or sensitive areas.</p></p>
